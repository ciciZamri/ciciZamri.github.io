Solving first-order Ordinary Differential Equations

Separation of variables
A technique that reduces the problem of solving certain first-order ODEs to evaluating two integrals. The equation can be written in the form of: $$\frac{dy}{dt} = g(t)f(y)$$ Step 1: \(\large \frac{dy}{f(y)} \normalsize = g(t)dt\)
Step 2: \(\large \int \frac{dy}{f(y)} \normalsize = \large \int \normalsize g(t)dt\)
