Online retail sector today

Mobile e-commerce become very popular nowadays as the cost to buy mobile devices such as smartphone keep decreasing every year. This allow any individual to have access to this technology. Online retail is the fastest growing retail channel. In 2017, it was reported that 78% of Internet users bought something online. Big data and artificial intelligence technology is also has changed online retail industry. Many online retail company were already adopting this technology to help operate and automate their business process. For example, they use the technology as a tool for predictive marketing. There are 7 major firm types in retail industry:

  • General merchandise
  • Durable goods
  • Speciality stores
  • Food and beverage
  • Gasoline and fuel
  • Mail order telephone order(MOTO)
  • Online retail firms

Challenges faced by different types of online retailers


Virtual merchants - Single channel web firms that generate revenues from online sales

  • building a business and a brand name quickly
  • many competitors in the virtual marketplace
  • substantial costs to build and maintain web application
  • considerable marketing expenses
  • large customer acquisition costs
  • steep learning curve

Multi-channel merchants - Have a network of physical stores as their primary retail channel but also begun online operations

  • High cost of physical building
  • High cost of large sales stuff
  • the need to coordinate prices across channels
  • the need to develop methods of handling cross-channel return from multiple locations
  • building a credible website
  • hiring new skilled staff

Catalog merchants - Have a national offline catalog operation as their largest retail channel but have recently developed online capabilities

  • High costs for printing and mailing
  • The need to leverage their existing assets and competencies to the new technology environment
  • the need to develop methods of handling cross-channel return
  • building a credible website
  • hiring new skilled staff

Manufacturer-direct merchants - Single or multi-channel manufacturers who sell to consumers directly online without the intervention of retailers

  • Channel conflict
  • Quickly developing a rapid-response online order and fulfillment system
  • switching from a supply push to a demand-pull model
  • Creating sales, service and support operations online
