Reflection on Netflix-Social Dilemma

Last updated: 20 October 2020 by Muhammad Hammizie

The Social Dilemma is a documentary film directed by Jeff Orlowski. This film revealed that popular social media applications such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram can be very harmful to the mental health of society. All these social media applications have millions of active users everyday around the world. It is true that this technology can bring a lot of positive impacts on society such as business marketing and long-distance instant message communications but most of the users do not realize the opposite side which are the negative effects.

In the film, it shows that there are a lot of privacy issues in social media applications. Incident of personal information data breaches is not a bug but it is one of the features of the system and this can be very disturbing to many users. Every activity or interaction with social media application such as watching videos, sending text messages, and like a friend's photo will be recorded. Then all the activities will be analyzed to create personalized experiences that can engage the individuals to use the app longer until at some point, the individual became addicted to use the apps. Social media apps also have sophisticated recommendation engine that can make the apps more addictive.

In conclusion, as a social media application user, we need to use the apps wisely so it can bring more goods than harm to us.
