CICI

Mart

Cicimart is a mobile application that allows users to buy groceries online and self-pickup their orders or get them delivered to their front door. This application also includes Scan and Go system that can offer the users to have faster checkout experience.

UI screenshots
Features

Product search

Users can find what they need using product search functionality.

Complete product information

Product information provided is complete and very detail.

Delivery service and self-pickup

Users can choose for delivery service or self-pickup.

Scan and Go

Get faster checkout experience and skip checkout line with Scan and Go system.

Barcode scanner

Use the mobile application to scan the product barcode to get the product information.

Reward points

Earn reward points by completing orders and claim amazing rewards from the application.
